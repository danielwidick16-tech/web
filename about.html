About Volcanic Heating & Air

Locally owned. Straightforward service. Built for Central Oregon.

Our Story

Volcanic Heating & Air is a locally owned HVAC company serving Bend and Central Oregon. We handle residential and commercial heating and cooling—service, repair, maintenance, and full system replacements. We started this company because we believed there was a better way to do this work: honestly, thoroughly, and with respect for the people we serve.

Our founder brings over a decade of hands-on HVAC experience across both commercial and residential systems. That means years of troubleshooting complex equipment, diagnosing problems others missed, and learning what actually works in the field—not just in a manual.

After working for larger HVAC companies, the pattern became clear: too much upselling, too many rushed jobs, and not enough straight talk with customers. We started Volcanic to break that pattern. Here, every diagnosis is honest. Every repair is done as if it were our own home. We explain what's wrong, what it costs, and what your options are—without pressure.

Built for Here
